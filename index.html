Apples

An apple is an edible fruit produced by an apple tree. Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the genus Malus.

Brigde

A bridge is a structure built to span a physical obstacle without blocking the way underneath. it is contructed for the purpose of providing passage over the obstacle.

Sunrise

Sunrise is the moment when the upper rim of the sun appears on the horizon in the morning. The term can also refer to the entire process of the solar disk.
